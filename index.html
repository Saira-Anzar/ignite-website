ABOUT

IEEE, and why IGNITE exists

IEEE

IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) is the world's largest technical professional organization, advancing technology for the benefit of humanity through publications, conferences, and community-driven standards.

IEEE Student Branch, LBSITW

Our Student Branch brings IEEE's global network to campus — running workshops, speaker sessions, and hands-on projects that help students build real technical skill outside the classroom.

Objective of IGNITE

IGNITE exists to give students a single space to learn new tools, test their skills under pressure, and meet people building in the same direction — across technical, professional, and interactive tracks.
